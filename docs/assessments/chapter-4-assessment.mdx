# Assessment Rubric: Chapter 4 - AI Integration and Intelligence

## Learning Objectives Assessment

### Objective 1: Design planning and reasoning systems for humanoid robots
- **Exemplary (4)**: Student designs sophisticated systems with advanced reasoning capabilities
- **Proficient (3)**: Student designs effective planning and reasoning systems
- **Developing (2)**: Student designs basic systems with functional reasoning
- **Beginning (1)**: Student struggles with planning and reasoning design

### Objective 2: Implement human-robot interaction interfaces
- **Exemplary (4)**: Student implements intuitive, sophisticated interaction interfaces
- **Proficient (3)**: Student implements effective interaction interfaces
- **Developing (2)**: Student implements basic interaction interfaces
- **Beginning (1)**: Student struggles with interaction interface design

### Objective 3: Apply autonomous learning and adaptation techniques
- **Exemplary (4)**: Student demonstrates advanced learning with sophisticated adaptation
- **Proficient (3)**: Student applies learning techniques with effective adaptation
- **Developing (2)**: Student applies basic learning techniques with some adaptation
- **Beginning (1)**: Student struggles with learning and adaptation

## Practical Exercise Assessment

### Hands-on Exercise Completion
- **Functionality**: Do the AI systems operate as expected?
- **Intelligence**: How well do the systems demonstrate intelligent behavior?
- **Interaction**: How effective are the human-robot interfaces?
- **Learning**: How well do systems adapt and improve over time?

## Performance Standards
- **A (90-100%)**: Meets all Exemplary criteria and demonstrates advanced understanding
- **B (80-89%)**: Meets most Proficient criteria with some Exemplary elements
- **C (70-79%)**: Meets most Developing criteria with some Proficient elements
- **D (60-69%)**: Meets Beginning criteria for most objectives
- **F (Below 60%)**: Does not meet Beginning criteria for most objectives

## Feedback Guidelines
- Provide specific feedback on AI system design quality
- Comment on the effectiveness of interaction interfaces
- Suggest improvements for learning and adaptation mechanisms
